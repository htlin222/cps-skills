## Multi-Symptom Mapping Rules

When a case presents with multiple symptoms, extract ALL relevant chapters:

- Chest pain + dyspnea: Ch 9 + Ch 15
- Fever + cough: Ch 10 (primary), consider Ch 5 if immunocompromised
- AMS + fever: Ch 11 + Ch 10, consider Ch 5
- Syncope + chest pain: Ch 31 + Ch 9
- Edema + dyspnea: Ch 17 + Ch 15
- Jaundice + abdominal pain: Ch 26 + Ch 3
- Diarrhea + fever: Ch 13 + Ch 10
- Hematuria + back pain: Ch 21 + Ch 7
- Weight loss + fatigue: Ch 32 + Ch 18
- Rash + joint pain + fever: Ch 29 + Ch 27 + Ch 10

Always include Ch 1 (Diagnostic Process) as a methodological reference.
