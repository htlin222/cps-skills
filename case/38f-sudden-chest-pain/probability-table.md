### The Microcytic Anemia Factor
- Hgb 9.0 + MCV 65 + RDW 18% = iron deficiency anemia
- **Exacerbates ischemia** via demand-supply mismatch (reduced O2 carrying capacity + physical exertion)
- Could be from menorrhagia (common in premenopausal women)
- APS association: antiphospholipid antibodies can cause thrombocytopenia but also associate with autoimmune conditions causing anemia
- Needs iron studies, reticulocyte count, peripheral smear
